About us

The meaning of "Blue Dream".

Blue symbolizes trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidience, intelligence, faith, truth and heaven.

We've decided to turn the symbol of blue color into a dream that is to be achieved. We intend to help people who are willing to survive and achieve their goals, especially the infants and the eldery people.

With this app, anyone who wants to contribute can help the people in need with just one click.

It will be demonstrated through a video that we will not keep the money we've collected, it will be used with a benifit for the needy people.
